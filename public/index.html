About Me

Hello! I'm César Albusac Jorge and I'm a Software Engineer mostly experienced in .NET solutions.

Since I was a kid, I saw my dad mounting computers and replacing pieces of them. I still have in my parents’ house one room with very old computers, monitors, keyboards, and dozens of floppy disks.

I started to be curious about how it was possible to access the information throw a screen or even text with people (I hate when somebody called the house telephone because cut the connection)

Although in my secondary school there was not a real Informatics subject as there are today, I started thinking about studying for a degree in it. A math teacher asked us in our last year in secondary school, what we would like to study when we finish. I remember it as it was yesterday. I said, "Computer Engineering", and he told me "Are you sure? That degree is very difficult, maybe you should have a look at different options".

Not only did I take the degree because I wanted it, but also it was a challenge to show that I was more than able. I finished my Degree, (living one year in Germany). Additionally, I finished two Master's Degrees, one in Computer Science and one for being a Secondary Teacher. During these years, I learnt different technologies and basics points of them: some program languages (C++, Java, C, Python), using of Databases (MySQL and NoSQL), the architecture of computers, use of Linux console, Cloud Computing (Azure and AWS), Web Applications (with Django), Android, etc.

Since I finished, I have enjoyed every job I have been. Learning from my direct managers and colleagues and doing my best. I visit them every time I have the chance. For me, working hours fly, and that's a good signal.

Another thing I love is discovering new countries. That's why I came to Ireland to work as a Software Developer. Apart from learning and practising English, I am learning from almost every person I meet here. Although people can think that the Irish are different from Spanish people, they are on the same friendly level! I love it.

I like to learn new technologies and try small projects on my own.Apart from the tech side, I like photography, play table tennis, pool and padel.
